About Us

The Stadium Chasers is a new and growing team based in Dublin, built by football fans for football fans. What began as a shared love for the game has evolved into a platform dedicated to helping supporters experience football beyond the screen. We specialise in creating carefully planned football travel packages, making it easier for fans to visit iconic stadiums and soak in the atmosphere that makes the sport so special.

At the heart of what we do is community. The Stadium Chasers is more than just travel. It’s a welcoming space where fans can connect, share experiences and feel part of something bigger. We pride ourselves on being approachable, inclusive and passionate about bringing people together through football, no matter who you support or where you’re travelling from.

Above all, we genuinely care about our customers. Every trip we help build is handled with attention, honesty and a passion for the experience. We listen closely, guide every step of the journey and most importantly always put the fan experience first. As we continue to grow, our goal remains simple: deliver unforgettable football experiences while building a strong, supportive community all while we chase stadiums together:)
